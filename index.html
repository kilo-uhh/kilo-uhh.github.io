What we study

Questions that drive our research

Can a satellite read the health of a reef?

We use hyperspectral and multispectral data to retrieve water quality, light, and shallow bathymetry from orbit, then test those readings against independent measurements.

How does the whole ahupuaʻa shape the sea?

The land and the ocean are one continuous system. We follow water from the watershed to the coast to ask how what happens mauka shapes the health of the reef and the kai.
