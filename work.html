How we work

We mend differences across perspectives through listening to communities, observing places, and building for our future together.

Listening to communities

We begin with relationships. Grounded in kuleana and reciprocity, we nurture our relationships with communities and listen to their needs and priorities.

Observing our places

Kilo is the practice of keen observation. We use satellite and in-situ measurements to observe places across scales, integrating what we see from orbit with what communities and in-situ instruments record.

Building for our future

We build accessible, open-hardware tools and pipelines with communities. What we make is meant to outlast any single project, growing kilo capacity for future generations.
